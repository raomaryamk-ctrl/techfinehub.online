How to save mobile data

Small habit changes can cut your monthly bill in half. Here are the tweaks with the biggest impact:

  • Lower video quality to 480p — instantly cuts streaming data in half or more.
  • Turn on Data Saver in YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Chrome.
  • Use Wi-Fi for big downloads, system updates, and Netflix binges.
  • Disable auto-play videos and auto-download media in WhatsApp.
  • Restrict background data for apps you don't need live.
  • Download music/playlists on Wi-Fi instead of streaming on mobile data.
  • Use the "Lite" versions of Facebook, Messenger and Instagram on slow days.

Biggest single winSwitching YouTube from 1080p to 480p can save you several GB a week with barely any difference on a phone screen.

Common data myths

  • "Closing apps saves data." Not really — background restrictions and lower quality save far more.
  • "WhatsApp uses tons of data." Only video calls do; texting is negligible.
  • "Bigger package = waste." Only if you don't use it — match it to your real GB.
  • "4G uses more data than 3G." Same content uses the same data — speed just delivers it faster.
  • "Airplane mode at night saves data." Modern phones already pause most background data; useful for battery, not data.
